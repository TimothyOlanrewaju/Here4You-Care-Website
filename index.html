About Here4You Care

At Here4You Care Ltd, we are more than a care provider - we are a trusted partner in enhancing the quality of life for individuals and families. Established with a focus on excellence, our company (registered number 15587616) operates from our central London office, delivering reliable and empathetic care across the UK.

We prioritise client satisfaction through innovative, person-centered approaches that respect individual preferences and promote independence. Our mission is to deliver exceptional, compassionate care that empowers individuals to live fulfilling lives in the comfort of their own homes or communities.

Our Mission

CQC Compliance

All our services are regulated by the Care Quality Commission, ensuring adherence to CQC Regulations 18 and 19 on staffing and support.

Our Staffing

In line with CQC Regulations 18 (Supporting Workers) and 19 (Fit and Proper Persons Employed), our staffing model emphasises qualified, vetted professionals. All caregivers undergo thorough background checks, mandatory training in safeguarding, and ongoing professional development. This ensures a skilled workforce capable of providing safe, effective, and responsive care.

Our Services

Here4You Care Ltd offers a comprehensive range of services designed to meet diverse needs. Each service is delivered by trained professionals, with care plans customised to individual requirements.

Domiciliary Care

In-home assistance with daily activities, personal hygiene, and medication management.

Sleeping/Waking Night Care

Overnight support to ensure safety and comfort, with options for vigilant monitoring or active assistance.

Supported Living

Independent living arrangements with on-site support for adults requiring minimal to moderate assistance.

Live-in Care

Round-the-clock care from a dedicated live-in caregiver, promoting familiarity and continuity.

Respite Care

Temporary relief for family caregivers, allowing time for rest while maintaining high standards of professional support.

Extra Care

Enhanced housing schemes with additional on-site services designed to promote greater independence and well-being.

Day & Evening Services

Flexible daytime or evening support for social activities, errands, appointments, or companionship when needed most.

Hospital Discharge Support

Coordinated care to facilitate smooth transitions from hospital to home, reducing readmission risks and ensuring continuity.

Palliative Care

Specialised end-of-life support focused on comfort, pain management, emotional care, and dignity in final stages.
